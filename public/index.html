What is this website?I’ve been making creative content for a long time. For almost as long as I’ve made it, that content has lacked a home. Whenever I’ve gone to share the works I feel comfortable sharing, it’s been linking people to files on Google Drive, and other silly things. No more. Something Whenever is a place for something whenever. That’s all. It could be blog style content, photos, videos, songs I’ve written, lyrics I liked, anything.
